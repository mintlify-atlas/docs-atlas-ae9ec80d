nHungry is a fast, mobile-friendly shopping list app built with Nuxt.js. Organize products by color-coded categories, assign them to specific supermarkets, browse alphabetically or by category, and sync your list across devices — all with offline support so it works even without an internet connection.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Visit [hungry.infoinnova.es](https://hungry.infoinnova.es) in your browser, or follow the [installation guide](/installation) to run it locally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first product">
    Tap the **Add** tab, choose a category and supermarket, type the product name, and tap **Add**. See [Adding products](/using-the-app/adding-products) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your shopping list">
    In the A–Z or Categories view, tap products to select them for your shopping list. Selected products appear in the **Cart** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shop and check off">
    Open the **Cart** tab at the store. Filter by supermarket, and tap each product to mark it as bought. When done, clear the list to start fresh.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Install nHungry as a PWA on your phone for the best experience — tap **Add to Home Screen** in your browser's share menu after opening the app.
</Tip>
