---
title: "Add products to your shopping list"
description: "Learn how to add products to nHungry's shopping list by selecting a category, choosing a supermarket, and entering a name in the Add tab."
---

The **Add** tab is your starting point for building a shopping list. Before a product can appear in your cart, you register it here — choosing its category and target supermarket so nHungry can route it correctly when you're in the store. Once added, the product sits in your catalogue and can be selected for any future shopping trip.

## Add a product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add tab">
    Tap the **+** icon in the bottom navigation bar. The Add tab opens with the category picker pre-selected on the first visible category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a category">
    Scroll the horizontal color-coded category list and tap the category that best matches your product. The selected category is highlighted. Only categories marked as visible in **Config** appear here.

    <Tip>
      Long-press any category tile to rename it. A text input dialog opens where you can type a new name. The change is saved immediately and reflected across the entire app.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a supermarket">
    Use the dropdown below the category list to select the supermarket where you usually buy this product. The list shows only supermarkets currently set to visible in **Config**. Select **Cualquier Supermercado** if the product is not tied to a specific store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type the product name">
    Tap the text field and enter the product name. You can also press **Enter** on a hardware keyboard to submit without tapping the button.

    <Note>
      Product names are limited to 30 characters. The input field enforces this limit automatically.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add">
    Tap the **Añadir** button. The button is disabled while the name field is empty, preventing accidental empty submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the notification">
    A success notification appears at the top of the screen confirming the product name, its category, and the assigned supermarket. The name field clears automatically so you can add another product right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after adding

The product is saved to local storage immediately. It appears in the **A–Z** and **Categories** views, where you can tap it to add it to your active shopping cart. The product starts with `selected: false` and `done: false` — it is in your catalogue but not yet on any shopping list.
