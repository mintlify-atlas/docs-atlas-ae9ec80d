---
title: "Browse and edit your product list"
description: "Explore how the A–Z and Categories views work in nHungry, and how to select products for your shopping cart or edit their details."
---

nHungry gives you two ways to browse your product catalogue: an alphabetical A–Z list and a category-grouped view. Both let you tap a product to add it to your active shopping cart. The A–Z view also includes an edit button so you can update a product's name, category, or supermarket without deleting and re-adding it.

<Note>
  Only products belonging to visible categories appear in either view. If a product seems to be missing, check that its category is enabled in **Config**.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="A–Z view">
    The A–Z view displays every product in your catalogue sorted alphabetically. As you scroll, a floating letter indicator appears briefly in the center of the screen to show which part of the alphabet is currently in view.

    **Selecting a product for the cart**

    Tap any product row to toggle its selection. A selected product (`selected: true`) moves to your **Cart** tab so it shows up on your next shopping trip. Tap it again to deselect it and remove it from the cart.

    **Searching**

    Tap the magnifier icon in the top-left corner to open a search field. Type any part of a product name — the list filters in real time, ignoring accents and case differences. Tap the × button or the magnifier again to clear the search and restore the full list.

    **Editing a product**

    Tap the edit icon next to any product to open the edit modal. The modal lets you change:

    - **Name** — update the product's display text (up to 30 characters)
    - **Category** — reassign the product to a different color-coded category
    - **Supermarket** — change which store the product is assigned to

    Tap **Guardar** to save your changes. If nothing changed, a notification confirms that no edits were made. Tap **Cancelar** to close the modal without saving.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Categories view">
    The Categories view groups products under their assigned category, with each group header showing the category's background color. Within each group, products are sorted alphabetically.

    **Selecting a product for the cart**

    Tap any product row to toggle its selection, exactly as in the A–Z view. The selected state is shared — a product selected here also appears in the **Cart** tab and vice versa.

    **Searching**

    The same magnifier search is available in this view. Filtering works across all visible categories at once; matching products remain under their respective category headers.

    **No edit button**

    The Categories view does not show an edit icon. To rename a product or change its category or supermarket, switch to the **A–Z** view.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Product count indicator

Both views display a counter in the top-right corner showing how many products are currently selected across your entire catalogue. Tapping this counter toggles the search field open or closed.
