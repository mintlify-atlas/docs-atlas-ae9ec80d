---
title: "Register and log in to nHungry"
description: "Create a free nHungry account, sign in, and manage your session to enable cloud sync across all your devices — registration is optional."
---

An nHungry account lets you sync your shopping list, products, categories, and supermarkets across multiple devices. Once you're logged in, a **Sync Data** button appears in the **Config** tab so you can push and pull your data at any time.

<Note>
  An account is entirely optional. nHungry works fully offline and without registration — you only need an account if you want to sync data across devices.
</Note>

## Create an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Config tab">
    Tap the **Config** tab at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the login dialog">
    Tap the **Register / Log In** button. A dialog will appear with the title "Iniciar Sesión" (Log In) and an email and password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Register mode">
    Tap the **Register** toggle link inside the dialog. The title changes to "Realizar Registro" and the confirm button changes to **Registrarse**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type your email address and choose a password that meets the requirements below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Registrarse**. If registration is successful, you'll see a confirmation and the app will log you in automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Your password must be at least 8 characters long and contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one digit. Passwords that do not meet these requirements are rejected before the request is sent.
</Warning>

## Log in to an existing account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Config tab">
    Tap the **Config** tab at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the login dialog">
    Tap **Register / Log In**. The dialog opens in Log In mode by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type the email and password you registered with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept">
    Tap **Accept**. On success, the dialog closes, your session is active, and the **Sync Data** button becomes visible in the **Config** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log out

Tap the **Log Out** button in the **Config** tab. A confirmation dialog will appear — confirm to end your session. Your local data is preserved; only the session token and email are cleared. Your device fingerprint is kept so the server can still recognise the device on your next login.

## Forgot your password?

A "Forgot password" link is present in the login dialog. This feature is not yet implemented — tapping it has no effect. Password recovery is planned for a future release.
